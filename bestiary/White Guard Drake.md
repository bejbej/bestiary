> **Multiattack.** The drake attacks twice, once with its bite and once with its tail.

> 
> **Tail.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage.
